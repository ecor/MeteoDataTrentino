Welcome to MeteoDataTrentino R package

The weather and snow data are provided by the stations of the Meteorological Service of the Province of Trento (www.meteotrentino.it) as Open Data through the following through the . Open Data portal . The downloaded can be coerced as SMET objects and imported within an R environment

Download and Installation

Form the R console, MeteoDataTrentino can be installed dirlectly from Github making use of devtools packagethe following commands:

library(devtools) install_github("ecor/MeteoDataTrentino")
